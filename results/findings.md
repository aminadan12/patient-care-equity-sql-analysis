# Findings

## Metric 1: Delayed Access to Care
- Average wait time from registration to first completed appointment was 345 days
- Women experienced longer average wait times than men
- Patients aged 35–49 had the highest average delay

## Metric 2: Drop-Off and Disrupted Appointment Attendance
- Overall drop-off rate was 34%
- Men had slightly higher no-show rates than women
- Men were much more likely to be repeat no-shows
- Patients aged 65+ had the highest no-show rate
- MedCare Plus patients showed the highest no-show rate by insurance provider

## Metric 3: Financial Friction in Billing
- Among finalised bills, 51.1% failed
- Failed payment rates varied strongly by insurance provider
- HealthIndia had the highest failed payment rate
- Failed payments increased with age, especially among ages 50–64 and 65+

## Recommendations
- Collect more qualitative data to understand causes of delays and no-shows
- Provide targeted reminders or transport support for vulnerable groups
- Investigate affordability and insurance-related barriers more closely
